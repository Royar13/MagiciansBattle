Lore

Teams of wizards and magical beasts compete in the greatest tournament in the world of magic - MagiciansBattle - to prove they are the very best.

Join one of the four factions, assemble your own legendary team and lead it to victory against the mighty opponents that await you in the battle arena!

Cards

Collect wizard, creature, spell and item cards to build the most fearsome and dominating deck in MagiciansBattle.

Beat your foes in battle to gain new cards (for free!) and trade cards with other players to perfect your deck.

Strategy

MagiciansBattle is a PvP, deck-building, turn-based strategy game on a hex grid map.

Every card offers rich possibilities of play, resulting in a very dynmaic and versatile game.

Test your skills, wit and courage in a fast paced and challnging strategic duel!
